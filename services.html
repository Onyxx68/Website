Our Services

Custom Built Guitars

At Gorka Guitrs we are focused at bringing you the best posible guitar that you will love for the rest of your life. Thats why we offer you the ability to customize your guitar fully to your liking.

With the options to change just about everyting on the guitar and even the type of wood for the neck and body is something that you can choose.

customizing a guitar with that much freedom can be alot, thats why we have the defult set up for each guitar that tries to get you the best of everything. If you need help deciding on parts for the guitar our small team is the perfect people to ask so you get exactly what you are looking for.

Guitar Restoration

If you happen to come across an old guitar and want it to be broght back to life again, we can help with that.

We offer you the ability to get us the guitar either, you can either ship it to us or you can drop it off at our location, located a half hour drive out of Ottawa inf the Wakefield QC regin.

We take you guitar and give it a full restoration, with options to save the original paint, and refinish the neck. We do it all to make the guitar look like it did when it first made, but still feel like the guitar that has been played and loved for decades.

Revamp your guitar

If all of those options are out of your budget, we also offer the option to revamp your guitar. Much like the restoration, we take the guitar and we will upgrade parts that you choose with the help from out guitar builders because we want you to have a guitar that you will love.

The options start at just a basic cleanup of the guitar and a resauder of all the parts, which can make years of a difference to its sound. Then we get into the fun stuff. You can pick all the parts that you want to put on your guitar. Every single part. Then our team will assemble it and either ship it back to you, or deliver it localy.
