About Us

Who We Are

We are Gorka Guitars, we are a small independent company that exclusively makes state of the art guitars. The guitars are innovative and are not the same as all the other custom guitars out there.

Our Main Goals

  • 1. To innovate what is possible with modern guitars.
  • 2. Make guitars that are 100% custom to you.
  • 3. Make high end guitars easily accessible to everyone

Where to Find Us

You can find us online here, and on our social media pages. We currently do not offer a store that you can come and visit but you can reach out to us via the "Contact Us" page if your interested in trying one of our guitars. You can either come to where we make the guitars in the Wakefield, Quebec area or we can drive out to you if your in the Ottawa-Gatineau area.
