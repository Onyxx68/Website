Our Best Seller

The Black Hole Guitar

The Black Hole Guitar is our original guitar that everyone wants. the guitar came to life over the last few years, with a single creator behind the design. With its custom inlays fretmarkers, to the handbuilt body, what is there not to love about this guitar?

The Guitar has a lot to offer with its large custamization that we are working very hard to offer to you. The guitar id not limited to any genra either, the guitar was desinged with many genras in mind. From the blues to death metal to lofi, the guitar will find its place very nicely.

The neck humbucker is ideal for the classic sounds of lofi, and if your feeling the blues, out push pull pots alow you to use only a single coil pick to get that classic blues sound. The bridge pickup is nice and dirty, ideal for heavy rock when the gain is cranked but will play very clean low. Same with the neck pickup, the bridge has a push pull pot aswell alowing you to get a more classic strat sound from the 80's

Who We Are

Gorka Guitars is a small company that was started up late 2024 by Alexandre Gorka. The team is very small and we are constantly working at improving the quality of our guitars.

The company is currently based out of Wakefield Quebec, Canada, a short drive out of the nations capital, Ottawa. We are currently trying to expand in to the Ottawa-Gatineau area.

What We Offer

Currently we offer a small variation of guitars, from guitars for lofi, blues, metal and hard rock. We are trying our best to bring you the ability to fully customize the guitar that you want to buy so that it is yours and yours allown.

If you are in the Ottawa-Gatineau area, we also offer guitar tune ups and guitar upgrades.
